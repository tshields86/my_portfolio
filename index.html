The Watchlistmore_vert
The Watchlistclose

The Watchlist is a personal finance portfolio tracker with social media applications developed with Ruby on Rails. Through Devise, users can sign up and have login/logout functionality. It includes a profile page with a listing of all the stocks a user is tracking. Users can search for new stocks to track by entering in the ticker symbol of the company and add the stock to their portfolio to begin tracking. It utilizes AJAX with the stock_quote gem to search for and display data. Users can also search for other users by first name, last name, email, or partial queries and view their Watchlists and add them as friends. These one-to-many and many-to-many relationships persist within a PostgreSQL database.
